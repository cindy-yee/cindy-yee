About

UrbanElite Pilates was created with you in mind. We understand with the current busyness in our city life, you are constantly looking out for fast and effective solutions for your fitness goals. We hear you.

In UrbanElite Pilates, we customised movement that DISRUPT (poor posture, compensation patterns and movement dysfunction), CREATE (to educate and customised exercises to optimise results) and REDEFINE (building on good awareness in executing your exercises with precision) to bring you to your ultimate success. Remember, NO ONE SIZE FITS ALL. To be effective, you have to be different, because you are unique.
