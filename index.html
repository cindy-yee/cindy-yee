What is Pilates?

Pilates is an exercise system created by Joseph Pilates in the 1920s. This unique exercise system emphasised on working the entire body uniformly on the mat and a variety of Pilates equipment that was invented by Joseph himself. It challenges the individual to improve body alignment and performance through the correct muscles firing pattern. This in turn, creates great flexibility, power and strength.
